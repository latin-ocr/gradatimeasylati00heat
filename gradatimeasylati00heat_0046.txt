THE RELATIVE.

ξ 21. (a) The RELATIVE is used to avoid repeating
a word (called its Antecedent) already used once.
Video murum, quem Balbus aedificavit.
I see the wall, which Balbus built.

If there were no Relative we should have to say,

Video murum, et Balbus eum murum aedificavit.
I see tlte wall, and Balbus built chat wall.

Thus it has also the force of a Conjunction, and
serves to connect Sentences.
